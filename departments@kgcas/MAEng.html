MA English

Department of English

Profile

The Department of English is active since KGCAS has been established in 2005. The Department offers Part II General English for the undergraduates. The College introduced English Literature as an undergraduate course in 2010 and post graduate course in 2014.
It has a pivotal role to play in the college, providing the means for students to reach an advanced level of proficiency in English as a support subject for other departments, as well as to promote a greater sensitivity to the literary and cultural traditions of the language.
