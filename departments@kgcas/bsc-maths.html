B.Sc Maths

Bachelor of Science in Mathematics

Profile

B.Sc Mathematics is an undergraduate degree course. The duration of the course is 3 years and divided into 6 semesters. Subjects generally studied under this program are Trigonometry, Algebra, Calculus, Vector Calculus, Differential Geometry, Graph Theory, Complex Analysis, Real Analysis, Differential Equations, Lap Lace Transforms, Statistics for Mathematics, Numerical Methods, Discrete Mathematics and different theories in mathematics.
