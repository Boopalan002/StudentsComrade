B.Sc Biotechnology

Department of Biotechnology

Profile

Biotechnology is the application of scientific, medical and engineering principles to the processing of materials by biological agents to provide goods and services to the mankind. Biotechnology plays a major role in serving mankind by contributing to industries like Agriculture, Animal Husbandry and Health Care. With this view in mind and aim to impart knowledge to the future generations and mould them to face the challenges in the trends of modern science.
