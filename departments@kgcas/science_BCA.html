BCA

Department of Computer Applications

Profile

The Department was started in the year 2005. The Bachelor of Computer Applications (BCA) is a 3-year (6 semesters) undergraduate program affiliated to Bharathiar University and recognized by the Government of Tamilnadu.
The course creates highly skilled, adaptable graduates who are able to design computer-based solutions to address information management and processing complications in industry, commerce, science, entertainment and the public sector.

Further study

After completing BCA, one can also opt for higher education. Although there is no dearth of opportunities for BCA graduates, be it the government sector, public sector or private sector, there are many higher degree programmes available for students with a Bachelors degree in Computer Applications like

Master of Business Administration (MBA)

Master of Computer Applications (MCA)

Master of Science in Computer Science (M.Sc CS)

Information Security Management

Master Degree in Information Management

Master of Computer Management
