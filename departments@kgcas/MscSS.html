M.Sc SS

Department of Software Systems

Profile

The Department of Software Systems was established in 2006. M.Sc. Software Systems degree program was introduced in the year 2007 to admit the first batch of the M.Sc. Software Systems students.
The department has completed 14 years of noble service in the field of higher education. In the year 2021. M.Sc. Data Analytics is introduced with the latest curriculum as per the requirements of the Software Industry.
