B.Com.PA

Bachelor of Commerce in Professional Accounting

Profile

B.Com Professional Accounting is a three-year undergraduate degree course that deals with the study of accounting, finance, marketing, economics, management, and other financial matters. The main aim of the course is to train the students and make them excellent both in theoretical and practical aspects related to financial analysis, creative thinking, good communication and interpersonal skills, and good mathematical skills and account knowledge.

Career Opportunities

Auditing Clerk

Accountant

Director of Accounts

Audit Manager

Professional Accounting Associate

Further study

After completing B.Com.PA, one can also opt for higher education. Although there is no dearth of opportunities for B.Com.PA graduates, be it the government sector, public sector or private sector, there are many higher degree programmes available for students with a Bachelors degree in Commerce in Professional Accounting like

Master of Commerce(M.Com)

Master's in Business Administration(MBA)

Certified Management Accountant(CMA)

Chartered Accountant(CA)
