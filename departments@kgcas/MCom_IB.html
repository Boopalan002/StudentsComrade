M.Com.IB

Master of Commerce in International Business

Profile

Master of Commerce in International Business, also known as M.Com IB, is a postgraduate degree course that focuses on business strategies concerned or sensitive to international relations or issues. M.Com in International Business programme prepares students for a career with increased responsibility focusing on multicultural concerns, diversity and international business strategies.
