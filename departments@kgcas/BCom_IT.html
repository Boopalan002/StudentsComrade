B.Com.IT

Bachelor of Commerce in Information Technology

Profile

The Department of Commerce IT has started in the year 2008. The course provides holistic knowledge of IT and computer science and its application in the commercial domain to our students. It aims at providing a complete overview of all the aspects related to the subject. This has now fuelled the demand for professionals who have good knowledge of commerce as a subject as well as a good command over IT-related skills.

Career Opportunities

System Analyst

Financial Information Analyst

Business Analyst

Assistant Auditor

Probationary officer

Software Development Firms

Further study

After completing B.Com.IT, one can also opt for higher education. Although there is no dearth of opportunities for B.Com.IT graduates, be it the government sector, public sector or private sector, there are many higher degree programmes available for students with a Bachelors degree in Commerce in Information Technology like

Master in Financial Management (M.F.M)

Master in Management Studies (M.M.S)

Master in Retail Management (M.R.M)

Master of Business Administration (M.B.A)

Master of Marketing Management (M.M.M.)

Master in Retail Management (M.R.M)
