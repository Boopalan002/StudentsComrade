B.Sc.CS

Department of Computer Science

Profile

The Department was started in the year 2005. The Bachelor of Computer Science (B.Sc. CS) is a 3-year (6 semesters) undergraduate program affiliated to Bharathiar University and Recognized by the Government of Tamilnadu. The course forges the students to develop various skills in technology, communication, interpersonal and presentation skills to meet the current trends in the real world.

Application Analyst

Web designer

Network Administrator

Further study

After completing B.Sc.CS, one can also opt for higher education. Although there is no dearth of opportunities for B.Sc.CS graduates, be it the government sector, public sector or private sector, there are many higher degree programmes available for students with a Bachelors degree in Computer Science like

Master of Computer Applications (MCA)

Master of Business Administration (MBA)

Master of Science in Computer Science (M.Sc CS)

Master of Computer Management
