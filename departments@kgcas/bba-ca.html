BBA CA

Department of Management Education

Profile

Bachelor of Business Administration Computer Application often termed as BBA CA is a three-year undergraduate program which covers all aspects of business and computer knowledge. The degree aims to focus on teaching students the fundamentals of business and management principles through classroom lectures, case studies, industry visits and internships and some computer languages

Career Opportunities

Sales Executive

Marketing Executive

Management Trainee

Market Research Analyst

Further study

After completing BBA CA, one can also opt for higher education. Although there is no dearth of opportunities for BBA CA graduates, be it the government sector, public sector or private sector, there are many higher degree programmes available for students with a Bachelors degree in Business Administration Computer Applications like

Master of Business Administration (MBA)

Master of Management Studies (MMS)

Post Graduate Diploma in Management (PGDM)

Post Graduate Program in Management (PGPM)

Chartered Accountant (CA)

Union Public Service Commission (UPSC)
